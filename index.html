About The One Ring

The one ring is a powerful ring created by Sauron to help him control the other rings and the ones that wore them.

He forged it in Mount Doom in Mordor.
When he created it he put a greater part of his power and will into the ring and it gained a will of its own.

The ring can move on its own. It can control or let the bearer control the wearers of the other rings that were already corrupted by Sauron's power.
The ring also corrupt's the bearer.
The bearer is invisible when wearing the ring and is also basically immortal but he is slowly tuning permanently invisible.

If someone strong enough wore it he wold have been corrupted and turned into the new Dark lord.
It is also nearly indestructible, the only thing in the Middle Earth that can destroy it are the fires of Mount Doom.
But since it has its own will the wearer would have had to have a really strong will to be able to throw it in without help.

The ring was lost after it revealed Isildur to his enemies and which led to his death and after Sméagol also known as Gollum found it, killed his cousin and hid in the Misty Mountains.
The ring was found after nearly 500 years when it tried to escape and Bilbo Baggings found it.

When Bilbo took it he used it to escape the mountains and complete his journey.
He had the ring for 60 years and used it without knowing what it actually was and that is did more than just make him invisible.
When Gandalf the Grey fount out what it was he warned Bilbo against using it more than necessary.

After the 60 years he gave it to Frodo and traveled to Rivendell.
Frodo took the ring to the Mount Doom where Golumm bit of his finger and flew into the fires of Mount Doom destroying the ring.
Frodo had the ring for 17 years.

Powers

  1. Invisibility
  2. Immortality
  3. Control over other corrupted rings and their wearers
  4. Corruption
  5. Awareness of other corrupted rings
  6. Amplification of power
  7. Has a will

1. Invisibility
Makes the bearer invisible to mortal eyes.
Spiritual being like wraiths can still see the bearer.
Beings capable of seeing the spiritual world can see the bearer.

2. Immortality
The bearer is immortal.
The bearer stops aging.

3. Control over rings
If the bearer is strong enough he can control the other corrupted rings and their wearers.

4. Corruption
After a long time of continuous corruption the bearer either loses his physical form and become Sauron's follower
or if he is strong enough he will become the new Dark lord.

5. Awareness of rings
All corrupted rings are aware of each other when being used and can sens their location.

6. Amplification of power
The ring amplifies the power that the bearer already possesses.

7. Will
The ring has its own will.
It can change its size, move or even influence the bearers thoughts.
